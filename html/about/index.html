公司介绍

我们是谁？
成立于2003年，是广东第一批获广交会资质的公司，也是第一批获得香港会展中心的指定搭建商。
公司荣获中国展览馆协会二级资质企业、广东省甲级设计企业、广东省施工一级企业等多项荣誉称号。专业为企业品牌展厅、博物馆、纪念馆、规划馆、科技馆提供整体展陈策划、方案设计、模型制作、多媒体展示技术设计开发、施工安装等提供全方位综合性服务。我们始终秉承“只做有故事的设计”的核心理念，致力于成为国内乃至国际一流的设计服务公司，为顾客提供最专业和最优质的服务。
Who are we?
Founded in 2003, Guangdong is the first batch of Canton Fair qualification of the company, is also the first batch of access to Hongkong Convention and Exhibition Center designated contractors. The company has won many honorary titles such as China Pavilion Association, two qualification enterprises, Guangdong first class design enterprises and Guangdong construction first-class enterprises. As a professional brand exhibition hall, museum, memorial hall, museum, Museum of science and technology to provide overall planning exhibition, Chen Cehuai design, model making, multimedia display technology design and development, construction and installation to provide a full range of integrated services.
